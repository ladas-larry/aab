### Insurance guide updates

I sat down with my favourite insurance expert, and we reviewed each insurance guide. We clarified the limitations of each insurance, and picked solid options for English-speaking immigrants.

The short version:

- **Liability insurance**  
    It's the most important insurance after health insurance. If you don't have it, get it.
- **Legal insurance**  
    Optional, but good to have. Landlords and employers often take advantage of immigrants. Legal insurance reduces the cost of getting legal advice and enforcing your rights. You just "let your lawyer loose" without worrying about legal fees or your odds of winning. It has limitations, and there are cheaper alternatives.
- **Disability insurance**  
    If you can't work because of an injury, you still need to feed your family and pay your mortgage. This insurance makes sense if you are self-employed, or if you have obligations to other people.

Since 2025, I prefer curation over aggregation. I choose what works, instead of letting you do the work yourself. It makes some guides sound a little like a sales pitch, but it means you get something that works.
