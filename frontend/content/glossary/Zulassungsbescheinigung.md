The *Zulassungsbescheinigung* is a vehicle's registration papers. In Germany, this document has two parts. [The first part](/images/Zulassungsbescheinigung-Teil-1-front.jpg) (*Teil 1*) folds to the size of a business card. You must carry it with you. [The second part](/images/Zulassungsbescheinigung-Teil-2.jpg) (*Teil 2*) is the size of a sheet of paper. You must leave it at home.

You get a *Zulassungsbescheinigung* when you [register a vehicle in Germany](/guides/buying-used-car-in-germany#register-the-vehicle).

The *Zulassungsbescheinigung* replaces an older type of registration paper, the *[[Fahrzeugbrief]]*.
