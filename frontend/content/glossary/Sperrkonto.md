A blocked account (*Sperrkonto*) is a special type of bank account. It only lets you withdraw a fixed amount every month.

A *Sperrkonto* with €{{SPERRKONTO_AMOUNT|cur}} in it is a [proof of financial resources](/glossary/Finanzierungsnachweis). You can use it to apply for a [[National Visa]].
