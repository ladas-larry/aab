The job seeker visa is a type of [residence permit](/glossary/Aufenthaltstitel). It gives you 6 to 18 months to [find a job](/guides/find-a-job-in-berlin) after you graduate from a German university.[^1] When you find a job, you can start working immediately.[^2]

You must apply for a job seeker visa at the *[[Ausländerbehörde]]* in Germany.
