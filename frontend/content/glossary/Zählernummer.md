Each electricity meter has a unique number called the *Zählernummer*. It's written on the electricity meter.

When you [move to a new address](/guides/moving-in), you must share the meter reading with your electricity company. You must give the meter number (*Zählernummer*) and the meter reading (*[[Zählerstand]]*).
